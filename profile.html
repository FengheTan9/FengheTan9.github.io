Research

I'm interested in medical image processing, computer vision and pattern recognition. Much of my research is about medical ultrasound image generation, segmentation and distant domain transfer learning. Representative papers are highlighted.

Conference paper

CMU-Net: A Strong ConvMixer-based Medical Ultrasound Image Segmentation Network
Fenghe Tang, Lingtao Wang, Chunping Ning, Min Xian, Jianrui Ding
ISBI, 2023   International Symposium on Biomedical Imaging
code / arXiv

We propose a fully convolutional segmentation network (CMU-Net) which incorporates hybrid convolutions and multi-scale attention gate.

Journal paper

A Novel Distant Domain Transfer Learning Framework for Thyroid Image Classification
Fenghe Tang, Lingtao Wang, Chunping Ning, Min Xian, Jianrui Ding
Neural Processing Letters, 2022
paper

A novel transfer learning method with distant domain high-level feature fusion (DHFF) model is proposed.

Journal paper

Study on Financial Fraud Account Detection Based on Imbalanced Datasets
Fang Lu, Fenghe Tang, Junheng Huang, B Wang
Computer Engineering, 2021

The strategy uses the iForest algorithm for mixed sampling of the data to remove the majority of noisy data and reduce the difficulty of the classifier learning from the minor classes.On this basis, a random forest classifier is designed to implement the detection of the accounts involved in financial fraud.

Journal paper

Frequent path discovery algorithm for financial network
Fang Lu, Fenghe Tang, Junheng Huang, B Wang
Chinese Journal of Network and Information Security, 2019

A two-direction active edge searching method is proposed to solve the problem of evaluating the relationship strength between financial entities. And a breadth-first frequent path discovery algorithm with depth controlled is presented,with which the way how the financial flows is restored.
